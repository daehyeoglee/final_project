participant: subject id number
target_duration: duration of the target stimuli in both sequences (in seconds)
distractors_duration_2: duration of the distractors in the second sequence (in seconds)
distractors_duration_1:duration of the distractors in the first sequence (in seconds)
response: participant's response; 0 = target from first sequence is longer, 1 = target from second sequence is longer
response_RT: participant's reaction time (in milliseconds)
condition: experimental condition; control, ensemble or distractors
trial: trial number within condition and participant
